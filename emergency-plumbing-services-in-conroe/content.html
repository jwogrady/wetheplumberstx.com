While you can’t predict when a plumbing issue will happen, you can prepare for one. At We The Plumbers, we encourage every homeowner in Conroe to take a few simple steps to reduce damage and act quickly when the unexpected strikes:

  1. Know your main shut-off valve location. Turning off your water supply immediately can prevent flooding and property damage.

  2. Keep our number handy: Save 936-Plumber (936-758-6237) in your phone so you can reach us instantly when seconds matter.

  3. Schedule preventive inspections. Our maintenance visits identify leaks, corrosion, and weak points before they turn into emergencies.

  4. Watch for warning signs: Unexplained moisture, damp spots, or rising water bills can all signal early plumbing issues.

  5. Stay calm and act fast: Shut off the water, turn off electricity in affected areas if safe, and call We The Plumbers right away.
